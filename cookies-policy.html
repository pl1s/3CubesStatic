  • 1. What are cookies?

    • 1.1. Cookies are small text files that are sent to and temporarily stored on the device of each person visiting the Website from which the Website is accessed.
    • 1.2. The information collected by cookies on the Website helps to identify the visitor of the Website, save the history of the Website visited and adapt the content accordingly. The information collected by cookies allows Us to ensure your ability to browse more conveniently, provide you with attractive offers and learn more about the behavior of users of the Website, analyze trends and improve the Website, personal service and services provided by Us.

  • 2. How to manage cookies?

    • 2.1. By visiting the Website, you can express your consent by your active actions if you want to use cookies. If you do not give your consent to the use of cookies, cookies that are not of a technical nature will not be stored on your device.
    • 2.2. Please be informed that you can also manage and/or delete cookies according to your preferences. You can choose which cookies you want to accept and which you want to reject in your browser settings (Internet Explorer, Safari, Firefox, Chrome, etc.). You can delete any cookies that are already on your device, and most browsers can be set to prevent cookies from being stored. The location of these settings depends on the browser you are using.
    • 2.3. Please note that in most browsers it is possible to:
      • check what cookies are stored and delete individual cookies;
      • block third-party cookies;
      • block cookies from specific Websites;
      • block the sending of all cookies;
      • delete all cookies when You close Your browser.
    • 2.4. You can withdraw your consent to the use of cookies at any time by changing your settings and deleting the cookies you have saved. If you choose to delete cookies, please note that any options you have set will also be removed and the Website may not function or function properly. For these reasons, we do not recommend that you disable cookies when using the Website.
    • 2.5. To learn more about cookies and how to manage or remove them, you can visit: www.allaboutcookies.org.
